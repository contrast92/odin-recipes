Recipe for the Lasagna

In this recipe, we will be making a lasagna with a meat sauce. This will be all with homemade ingredients.

ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. cook the meat
    2. Cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly. Add the onion and continue cooking until it's translucent. Stir in the canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.
  2. cook the noodles
    3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add the lasagna noodles and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  3. Make the cheese layer
    4. Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Assemble the lasagna
    5. Layer the ingredients according to the recipe (starting with sauce and ending with mozzarella) until the lasagna is assembled.
  5. Bake the Lasagna
    6. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Cover the lasagna with aluminum foil. The Lasagna need to be cooked for about 50 minutes, 30-40 minutes covered with aluminum foil, and 5-10 minutes uncovered. Bake until the top is golden brown and the lasagna is bubbly.
